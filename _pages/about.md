_Sound designer, audio programmer & music tech educator._

Miguel Moreno is based in Monterrey, Mexico, where he studied B.S. in Music Production Engineering at TEC de Monterrey. With a focus in music technology, he has specialized in sound design and synthesis with open-source software, mainly Pure Data. He has given workshops, classes and presentations in spaces such as CENART, CCM and the Centro Cultural España in Mexico City.

Within his experience, he has developed and designed musical applications for various independent companies like Destroy All Circuits and Moon Echo Audio. As part of his work, he has collaborated with artists in sound art exhibitions presented at the MARCO museum and the Santa Lucía festival.

Moreno recently developed a library of open source synthesized instruments and some as audio plug-ins such as LIRA-8 and EP-MK1. He currently works as a professor of music technology at TEC de Monterrey.
